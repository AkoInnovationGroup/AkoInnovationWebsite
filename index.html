About Us

Ako Innovation Group is a leading technology and innovation holding focused on AI, robotics, software, education, and media. Originally founded as Ako Robotics, the company quickly gained recognition for its autonomous systems and AI-driven solutions, achieving multiple milestones in robotics and automation.

As Ako expanded, it evolved into a multi-sector innovation group, fostering and scaling high-impact ventures, including: Ako Robotics: Advancing robotics & AI technologies Palet: Revolutionizing architecture education & collaboration Tamam: AI-powered DevOps and HR platform for smart automation and team management IR-FORCE: Competitive student and junior robotics team building intelligent robots for international contests iCast: Next-generation podcast technology platform

With a strong presence in Iran’s top innovation hubs, Ako is committed to empowering startups, investing in emerging markets, and shaping the future of technology and entrepreneurship.

Our Subsidiaries

IR-FORCE

Fields of Activity

Robotics: Designing and manufacturing various robots for research, service, logistics, agricultural, and industrial applications.

Autonomous Systems: Developing cameras, sensors, LiDAR, and advanced algorithms for robots and autonomous vehicles relying on artificial intelligence.

Autonomous Vehicles: Designing intelligent vehicles with an emphasis on safety and application in urban and industrial environments.

Diverse Robotic Projects: From small-scale to large-scale industrial projects.

Vision and Goals

  • To become a market leader in designing and producing autonomous systems and advanced robots.

  • To expand new technologies and enter international markets.

  • To increase scientific and industrial collaborations for product development.

  • To provide practical and commercial solutions in the field of autonomous transportation.

Our Innovative Projects

1:10 Scale Autonomous Car
An intelligent 1:10 scale vehicle that navigates routes fully autonomously using machine vision, sensors, and advanced control algorithms.
Smart Drone
A flying robot with machine vision and autonomous flight algorithms, capable of identifying paths, obstacles, and ground targets in unknown environments.
Smart Energy Optimization Software
Based on IoT and AI, to reduce energy consumption in buildings and companies and automatically control equipment.
Smart Wheelchair for Quadriplegic Patients
Equipped with a multi-modal control system using touch and voice interfaces for comfortable, safe, and independent movement of patients.
Warehouse Robot
For moving goods in warehouses using sensors, line-following algorithms, and machine vision; capable of choosing the optimal transport route.
Smart Irrigation System
Based on IoT and smart solenoid valves, with soil moisture and air temperature analysis for automatic and optimal irrigation control, aiming for significant water consumption reduction.
